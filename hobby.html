Current interests

As I have gotten older, I have grown to prefer indoor activities, and here are my current interests:

  • Read novels

    • Each novel is like a small independent world. Whenever I read a novel, I always have a feeling of being there, which is very interesting.

  • Sleep

    • Lately I have been dreaming a lot while sleeping. It bothered me at first when this happened, since it often caused me to lose my energy. But then I found out that dreaming is an interesting thing, because things that are usually difficult to do can be easily done in a dream. And I always dream while I sleep, so I like to sleep.

  • Eat

    • For me, eating brings me happiness, and most of the time it costs less than other entertainment activities.
